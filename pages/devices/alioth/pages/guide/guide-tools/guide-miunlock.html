MiUnlock

1. Install drivers (Skipping this step you will not be able to continue);

2. Create a Xiaomi account and sign in on your smartphone;

3. On the smartphone, in the developer options, enable "USB Debugging" and "OEM Unlock";

4. Enter the option "Mi Unlock Status" and click on "Add account and device";

5. Download Miflah Unlock and extract the zip;

6. Restart your smartphone in Fastboot and connect it to your computer;

7. Run the . exe file, click "Unlock" and wait for the indicated time. After waiting repeat this step;

8. Finish!
